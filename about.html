ABOUT

JC R. Ubalde is a freelance photographer, videographer, and designer who thrives in environments where artistic visions come to life. Whether it's capturing moments through photography, designing compelling shirt mock-ups, logos, and packaging, or shooting films and videos for stores and shops, JC aims to create work that not only looks visually appealing but also resonates deeply with the audience.

He is proficient in a variety of creative tools, including Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI, Vegas Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro, Figma, CapCut, Filmora, and Blender. These tools enable JC to remain versatile and adaptable to various project needs, whether editing videos, designing graphics, or creating 3D visuals. Looking ahead, JC R. Ubalde is driven by the ambition to become a creative director who leads with imagination and innovation, embracing every artistic opportunity that comes their way. JC believes that the best work comes from being authentic and pushing the limits of what’s possible.

If you’re interested in collaborating or simply want to connect, feel free to reach out—I’m always excited to explore new creative opportunities!
